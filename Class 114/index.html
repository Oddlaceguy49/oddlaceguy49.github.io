Instructions

  • The mustache checkbox gives you a mustache
  • The clown nose checkbox will give you a clown nose. If you put your wrist near your nose, it will make a horn sound
  • The draw pose checkbox will toggle wether or not you want the pose lines
  • The shirt overlay checkbox will overlay an image on top of your shirt. You can upload your own image using the file upload button.
  • The keep ratio checkbox only affects the shirt overlay effect and will make the image keep its original ratio

    • Have Fun!!!
